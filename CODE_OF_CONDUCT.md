# Code of Conduct

The Chamorro Language AI Initiative is committed to fostering a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative environment for all contributors.

---

## 1. Our Standards

Contributors are expected to:
- Treat all participants with respect and professionalism.
- Use inclusive and culturally sensitive language.
- Welcome diverse viewpoints and constructive feedback.
- Focus on collaboration and shared goals.
- Respect cultural protocols related to the Chamorro language.

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
- Dismissive or hostile communication.
- Sharing copyrighted, sensitive, or culturally restricted materials.
- Disruptive or intentionally harmful actions.

---

## 2. Responsibilities of Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Reviewing contributions fairly.
- Enforcing this Code of Conduct.
- Removing or rejecting contributions that violate standards.
- Addressing issues respectfully and promptly.

---

## 3. Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness a violation:
- Open a confidential issue in the repository, or
- Contact a project maintainer directly.

All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately.

---

## 4. Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- Warnings  
- Temporary or permanent bans from contribution  
- Removal of content  

---

## 5. Acknowledgment

This Code of Conduct is adapted from widely used open-source community guidelines and tailored to the cultural context of the Chamorro language.

By participating, you agree to uphold these standards.
